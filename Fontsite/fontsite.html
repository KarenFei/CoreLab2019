This website was inspired by the photos that I collect from Chinatown. Most of the shop signs and advertisements are using both Chinese and English. It is interesting to see that different type of fonts in two language works together. This website collects both the images of combination of two languages we see on the street and on screen.

这个网站源自于我从中国城收集的图片，中国城大部分商店的招牌和广告都是由中文和英文两种语言组成。不同语言和不同种类的字体用于同一个商标或者在同一张广告上出现会有许多不同的效果。这个网站收集了我们在街上和在屏幕上看见的商标广告书籍等图片。
